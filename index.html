SAHARA

The Sahara Desert is a vast, arid expanse in North Africa, covering about 3.6 million square miles. It is known for its scorching temperatures, towering sand dunes, and minimal vegetation. The desert is home to various nomadic tribes and unique wildlife adapted to its harsh conditions. Its beauty and challenges make it an awe-inspiring natural wonder.
